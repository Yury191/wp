General

How is Central Park priced?
Our private tutoring offerings range anywhere between $135/hour for our most basic packages to $475/hour for our highest quality offering. While our SAT bootcamp costs $4,500 for 90 hours of instruction. We are unique in that all our packages come with 24/7 student question support (through video, audio, and chat sessions!) as well as dailycustomized practice problems through our Gramercy app.
What type of service does my son or daughter need?
Private tutoring or bootcamp? SAT or ACT? It all depends on your goals and your son or daughter's baseline. The complementary tutoring session is usually enough to pin down the right service.
How does your price compare to other tutoring services?
Our pricing is very competitive with other tutoring companies in New York. In addition to being competitive on price, you can actually monitor your son's or daughter's progress on our customized prep (with score estimates based on thousands of data points) through our automatically generated reports, so there is never a question of value.

Other tutoring companies will quote you a set rate for a fixed number of lessons over the course of a several months, and what happens inside that time is a black box - a tutor and your child may give you their opinion on how they are doing, but what actually happens on test day is anyone's guess. Central Park removes the guessing from the equation by having a student complete his practice questions through our mobile applications and overlaying his progress over thousands of data points of back data.
Are tutoring sessions locked into a set schedule?
Not at all! You can adjust your scheduled sessions at any time. Students can even order on-demand lessons, video chat, or call us at their convenience!
What is the average SAT score increase for your customers?
After 10 sessions, we have found the average score increase to be just over 270 points, with the biggest increase happening in math.
I was referred by a current or past customer. Do I get a discount?
You do! Both of you and your referrer get 20% off.
